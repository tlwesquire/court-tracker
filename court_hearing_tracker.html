💰 Fair Rate Analysis — All Hearings
Every hearing is assessed against a recommended minimum fee based on driving distance from your Oneonta office. You appear as local defense counsel in each case, contracted by Nationwide Appearance Attorneys. Bundled hearings (same day, same county) get a reduced bundle fair rate since mileage is shared across the trip.

📦 Bundled Hearings
Hearings on the same date at the same county courthouse share one drive. The lead hearing bears the full recommended rate (full mileage + appearance). Each additional hearing is priced at the zero-distance appearance rate only — you're already there, so no extra travel cost applies.
⚖ Creditor Analysis (Opposing Plaintiffs)
Hearings grouped by the creditor / plaintiff firm (the suing party). You appear on behalf of the defendant in each case, contracted through Nationwide Appearance Attorneys. This view shows which creditors are generating the most work, and whether you're pricing appropriately for each.

          Export your hearings as a .ics file — import into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook with one click.
